Who We Are

We believe in commitment, quality and motivation to serve our customers to realize their business into more world wide and strongly connected with business users.

Tekniti is a growing consulting and technology Services Company runs on technology intelligence and motivation to make this world more reachable, accessible to technology and a technical friendly place.

Our company is founded by the minds and vision of technical people having a decade experience in developing enterprise level applications, providing architectural solutions, managing teams.

Having quantity and quality technical people with professional experience, business experience, intelligence, talent and can do motivation, we are powered to serve wide range of business to provide technology, business, supporting and consulting services.

We can serve you world wide having our company branches at India, USA and expanding at a faster pace.

we are powered by a pool of talented IT and consulting professionals across more than fifteen different technologies including Java Enterprise solutions, Adobe CQ Content management solutions, supply chain management, financial management, client relationship management, business analytics, enterprise reporting , HR and human capital management, enterprise security and administration and web development amongst other capabilities.

We believe in the mantra that smart technology decisions make good career for consultants and as well for the enterprise. We believe that our biggest asset is our human capital and not the technological advantage. Hence our focus is on dedicated support for our consultants – whether it is training them, staffing them, helping them make better career choices or standing by them.
